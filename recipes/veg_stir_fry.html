Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

This vibrant vegetable stir-fry is a quick and healthy meal option, packed with colorful veggies and a savory sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, saut√©ing for 30 seconds.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and stir to coat the vegetables.
  5. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.
