Beef Tacos

Description

These tasty beef tacos are packed with flavor and topped with fresh ingredients, making them a perfect meal for any night!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 taco seasoning packet
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Sour cream and salsa for topping

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat, then add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions.
  3. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  4. Warm taco shells in the oven according to package instructions.
  5. Fill each taco shell with the beef mixture and top with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  6. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
